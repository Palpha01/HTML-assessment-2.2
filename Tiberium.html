In 1995, a meteor fell to Earth, near the Tiber River in Italy. The meteor was found to contain a mysterious alien mineral, which began to spread around the globe and was dubbed "Tiberium" for its place of discovery.

People soon realized its potential, both as an economic foundation and as a weapon. They also realized the dangers it posed. Three major wars over the alien crystal were fought between 2, eventually 3 and minorly 2 different world superpowers:

The UN-backed peacekeepers of Global Defense Initiative, the nomadic outcasts of the Forgotten, the tiberium aliens of the Scrin, the cybernetic army of CABAL, and the quasi-religious movement of the Brotherhood of Nod, in the name of Kane.
